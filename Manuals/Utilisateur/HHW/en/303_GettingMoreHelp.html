Getting More Help



1. Help Videos.
   
    In addition to this manual, the software is released with help videos.
You will find a list of those videos through the menu Help > Video Tutorials...

    Use the dual screens mode (View > Two Playback/Analysis Screens), put the video tutorial in one of the two screens and you working video in the other.
This way you can follow the guide at your pace. (Remember that you can easily slow down the video too.)
